Product details

Two ways to get a gang sheet.

Pick the sheet length first — the builder opens locked to that size for $0.60 over the flat sheet price. Or pick a width and length, upload one image, and the shop lays the sheet out for you.

Build it yourself. Pick a sheet type and length on this page — 22" or 46" DTF from $12.60, glitter from $12.60, glow from $18.60, or 24" sublimation from $9.10 — and the builder opens at exactly that size. Arrange your designs across the sheet: mix sizes, mix artwork, pack it as tight as you like. The size and price you picked are what land in the cart; we don't do rolling sheets or auto-calculated pricing.

Or let us build it. Pick a sheet width below, choose the length on that product page, upload one image, and add it to the cart — the shop lays out the gang sheet for you at the flat sheet price. Builder sheets run $0.60 over flat; if your job doesn't fit the fixed sizes, the contact form gets you a firm price before anything prints. Prefer to talk it through? Call (304) 688-9970.

Standard DTF sheets are warm peel — press, wait a few seconds, peel while warm. Glitter and glow are cold peel. Finished sheets ship nationwide or wait for pickup in Logan, West Virginia.
